Influence

D-core is a new influence metric representing jointly the authority and collaborative attitude of an author as it is extracted from the densest citation graph he/she participates, adopted by the aminer.org scientific portal. For more details see the full paper: D-cores: Measuring Collaboration of Directed Graphs Based on Degeneracy. Christos Giatsidis, Dimitrios M. Thilikos, Michalis Vazirgiannis. ICDM 2011: 201-210.
